  • About Kevin and Keeley

    Keeley and Kevin both grew up in the suburbs of Rochester, NY. While they did not know each other growing up, they did meet at Kevin’s college – SUNY Geneseo – through a mutual friend. They now both live in Boston, MA and, a year and a half ago, got engaged atop the Blue Hills Skyline hiking trail overlooking the Boston city skyline.

    Keeley and Kevin love the outdoors. They clearly love hiking, but they also love camping, biking, and beach-hopping. They even spend as much time outside as possible in the winter months – skiing, snowboarding, ice skating, etc.

    They spent their spring break 2017 spending most of their time outside in Nassau, Bahamas. They definitely miss the Bahamas already. However, they both look very much forward to going back to the Caribbean for their honeymoon.
